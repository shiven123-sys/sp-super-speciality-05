Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do I need a referral to book an appointment?
No, you can directly book via our form or WhatsApp — no referral required.
❓ What conditions do you treat?
We specialize in pediatric surgical conditions (e.g., hernias, undescended testis, neonatal anomalies), neurosurgical problems (e.g., brain/spine tumors, neurotrauma), and general surgical consultations for children and adults.
❓ How do I prepare for my first consultation?
Please bring any previous medical reports, scan images, and a list of current medications. Also carry your insurance card if you plan to use it. We recommend arriving 10‑15 minutes early to complete registration.
